  • 2011: We opened our first foreign office in 2011, and we were fortunate enough to have it in the wonderful country of Italy, in Milan.
  • 2012: We grew and opened our first global gym in the heart of a technologically advanced area. a center for innovation Osaka is a city in Japan.
  • 2012: We had a good year in 2012. We opened not one, but two gyms in the world's most flourishing cities. A fast-paced African country with a plethora of chances.
